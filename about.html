Welcome

Eugenia was born in 1994 in Rome and she graduated in Industrial Design and in Product Design at "La Sapienza" University of Rome. During her studies, she developed several skills that concretized in designing projects regarding many design fields. In the 2016 she spent six months in France for an Erasmus programme and during the 2017 in the Netherlands as well. These experiences together with the internships she had within a graphic studio, Ginko, and a famous italian company, Covo Srl, empowered her abilities and knowledge about the design discipline in the world. She took part in the Caravana Design Weijdstrid in the Netherlands as one of the ten contest finalists and she exhibited her project during the WTC Expo in the 2018. She completed the academic path with a thesis focused on the emotional memory and sensorial values of daily life objects, designing a writing desk that could well embodied these concepts. At this regard, she could have her design realized thanks to a famous and big carpentry of Rome, Antonacci Falegnamerie Srl. She is now searching for a new experience to professionally grow and deepen her passion and abilities in the field.
