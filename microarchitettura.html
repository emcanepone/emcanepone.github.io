HOME

Microarchitecture for "La Sapienza" University of Rome

Home was thought to keep a dialog with the Rationalism, integrating it with the modernity. We took ispiration by the Bourellec brothers’ works, especially the diaphram; the “refuge”, a concept that represents the “house” in the collective immagination; our project wants to be a landmark and a meeting point for the students, giving them the possiblity to be informed in a original and interactive way. The labyrinth was ideated to create a space that stimulates the student and amuses him. The fi rst ispiration comes from the labyrinth in the Palace Versailles gardens made by Andrè La Notre with a group of funtains and sculptures to tell the Esopo stories. We decided to place some QR CODE in specifi c corners around the labyrinth to inform the students about several themes. In the centre of this place, a transparent, minimal and squared fountain creates a meeting point to chill out and relax.
