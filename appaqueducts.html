AQUEDUCTS APP

Service Application for the Park

1.Audioguide
It allows to locate your position to chose the closest and easiest to follow. Clicking on the icons you can directly listen to the audioguide about the paths.
2.Fitness
This options allows to verify if in the park there will be some group sport activities taught by expert personal trainers giving the possibility to partecipate.
3. History
It allows to discover the park’s and aqueducts’ history giving the possibility to see several pictures related to the text.
4. Street food
This option allows to discover if in the park some street food event has been organized giving news about it.
