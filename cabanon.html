ALTEREGO

Cabanon kitchen

Alterego project was born after an accurate analysis and research about the Cabanon, Le Corbusier, the «new man» and the new society. We created a multifunctional, useful and minimal object. It is used as a sink or washbasin and you can put forms as cutting boards and colander to make it more effi cient. Moreover the two big cilinders are used as fitted conteiners. The project wants to re-think the furniture in a contemporary point of view, manteining as fixed points the typological structure and the dimensions. The Cabanon follow the principles of Razionalism, bare and essential. The new Cabanon follows as well these principles but translating them in the contemporaneity
