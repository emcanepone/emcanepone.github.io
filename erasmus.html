BRAND IDENTITY

Graphics for the Erasmus Office of "La Sapienza" University of Rome

During the time I spent in the Erasmus Office as a winner of the scolarship related with the collaborations of honours students with some Departments of the University, I could help in developing and improving the identity and the graphics of the Office itself and the great committment of the people working here. I was asked to realize Christmas and Easter brochures and greeting cards, logos for the merchandising and some projects for the Incoming students and so on. It was a great opportunity to try my skills and facing real tasks.
