CLAP

Foldable Table

Clap has been developed during my Erasmus programme in France. We have been asked to produce an object to fit in a small kitchen, meeting the actual necessity to save space because of the increasingly small spaces in which we live. My key words for this project were: dinamicity, praticality, simmetry. I designed a table to be practical, compact and easily removed to save space. Thank to a wooden joint, it can be opened and closed depending on the uses. When closed, it becomes a simple and comfortable shelf to put things on, have a tidier kitchen and optimize the space in the room. It has been made with wood and plexiglass, manually processed to become translucid and reach that particular texture.
