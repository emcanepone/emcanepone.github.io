GNAM

Rome National Modern Art Museum

GNAM logo was born after a reflection about art and modernity. Art is movement because of its currents succession, and it is a very fast process. So we have chosen the circle. The M is emphasized because the modern is something that unhinge the art standards. The type is irregular and there is a dynamic play with thicknesses and spots: they are also in the circle to platonically join the modernity with the art which everyday leave their tracks (the spots) inside all of us. So we decided to put spots around the logo too and in this way they become an important element of the brand identity.
