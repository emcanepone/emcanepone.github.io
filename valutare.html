VALUTARE AGRICOLO

eBook Illustration for Arch. Aldo Canepone

This logo was designer for the book “Valutare Agricolo” by Arch. Aldo Canepone for the book editor Timia, in which it is discussed the topic of the technical and evaluated value of the farms, even their market value. I decided to stylize the first letters of the title giving them a dynamic shape remanding a bale of hay with the “V” embracing it to analyze it and estimate its values.
